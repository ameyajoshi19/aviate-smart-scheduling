Aviate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Aviate mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Aviate is designed to work primarily on your device. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Task data — titles, priorities, deadlines, durations, and labels you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences & settings — theme, availability, and scheduling preferences you configure. Stored locally on your device.
  • Account information (optional) — if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from that provider solely to identify your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display and schedule your tasks within the App.
  • To sync your preferences across sessions if you are signed in.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Task data and preferences are stored locally on your device using the device's secure storage mechanisms. We use industry-standard practices to protect any data transmitted to our servers. No sensitive personal data is transmitted without your action.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with the following third-party services at your option:

  • Google Sign-In / Google Calendar — governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We only access calendar data you explicitly authorize, and only to display and schedule tasks around your existing events.

5. Children's Privacy

Aviate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Delete all your task data by uninstalling the App.
  • Revoke Google or Apple sign-in permissions through your device settings.
  • Request deletion of any account-associated data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
